GDPR

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data controller

Quaerens Ltd is the data controller for personal data collected through ExitMyTimeshares.com unless stated otherwise.

2. Types of data

We may process identity data, contact data, order data, correspondence, uploaded documents, technical data and usage data connected with your enquiry or purchased service.

3. Purpose of processing

We process data to provide services, handle enquiries, manage orders, organise documents, communicate with you, improve our website and meet legal or regulatory obligations.

4. Your GDPR rights

You have the right to access, correct, erase, restrict, object to processing, request portability and complain to a supervisory authority. Some rights are subject to legal limits.

5. Consent

Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect processing that took place before consent was withdrawn.

6. Third parties

We may use trusted suppliers for hosting, payments, email, analytics, document handling and professional support. These parties only receive information needed for their role.

7. International users

If you contact us from outside the UK, your data may be processed in the UK or by service providers operating in other jurisdictions with appropriate safeguards where required.

8. Security

We use access controls, secure systems and practical safeguards to reduce the risk of loss, misuse or unauthorised access.

9. Retention

Data is retained for the period needed to deliver the service, manage records, meet legal requirements and deal with disputes or complaints.

10. Contact

GDPR enquiries can be sent to info@quaerens.co.uk.
