A timeshare cancellation request needs a careful paper trail

Many owners start by calling the resort and are told there is no way out. A written approach is usually stronger because it records what you asked for, why you asked for it and how the company responded.

The DIY kit helps you prepare cancellation wording, refund review wording, settlement requests and follow-up letters. It is designed for owners who want to start without paying thousands to an exit company.

You can still choose the full-service route if you prefer help preparing and managing the documents.

Reasons owners often seek cancellation

Maintenance fees

Annual charges keep increasing while the value or usefulness of the timeshare falls.

No realistic resale

Many owners discover that the timeshare has little or no resale market.

Sales concerns

Promises about investment value, exchange options or easy resale may not match reality.
