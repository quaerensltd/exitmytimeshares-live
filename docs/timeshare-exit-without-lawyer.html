DIY route

DIY does not mean unsupported or unstructured

A DIY timeshare exit route means you prepare and send the documents yourself, using guidance and templates instead of starting with a high upfront professional fee.

The aim is not to pretend every case is simple. The aim is to give you a sensible starting route: organise the evidence, send a clear request and track the response.

If the matter becomes harder or the resort refuses to engage, ExitMyTimeshares.com can assist further under the support route explained on the site.

What the DIY approach helps you do
