When the resort says no

A refusal should be handled calmly and in writing

Some resorts respond to exit requests with a short refusal, a generic policy answer or a request for more fees. Others do not respond properly at all.

The DIY kit includes follow-up wording so you can ask for a proper explanation, challenge unclear replies and keep the paper trail intact.

If the timeshare company does not accept the documentation, ExitMyTimeshares.com will take over and assist. In the rare situation where we cannot get any further, you will be fully refunded.

Common resort responses

No exit policy

A resort may say it does not offer exits, without considering your specific situation.

Pay more fees first

Some providers demand arrears, admin fees or transfer charges before discussing release.

No proper reply

Silence or generic replies can make a documented follow-up route important.
