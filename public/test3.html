KNOW YOUR RIGHTS

Many timeshare owners have the legal right to cancel their contract — especially in the EU and parts of the US. Even if the cooling-off period has passed, you may still be able to exit based on unfair terms, lack of transparency, or misrepresentation.

  • EU Timeshare Directive 2008/122/EC: Grants cancellation rights for lifetime contracts, lack of clear info, or pressure tactics.
  • Spanish Supreme Court rulings: Declared many contracts void if signed without proper legal compliance.
  • UK & USA: Varies by state — but many resorts accept cancellation letters or voluntary surrenders if done properly.
  • You are not required to use an expensive lawyer or exit company — a properly written letter is often enough.
