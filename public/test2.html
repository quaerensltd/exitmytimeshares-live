Know Your Rights!

Many timeshare owners have the legal right to cancel their contract — especially in the EU and parts of the US. Even if the cooling-off period has passed, you may still be able to exit based on unfair terms, lack of transparency, or misrepresentation.

  • EU Timeshare Directive: Right to cancel within 14 days of purchase.
  • Spanish Law: Contracts signed without clear info may be void.
  • US & UK: Many resorts accept voluntary surrenders—no lawyer needed.
